Refer & Earn

What is it?

Everytime you refer someone, you receive ₹20 credit on their 1st purchase of ₹99 or above. These credits never expire and there's no limit on how many people you can refer!

How it works?

1. You refer Pixello Services to your friend.
2. They mention your WhatsApp number at the time of purchase.
3. That's it! You will receive the refer credit automatically.



Terms & Conditions

1. Valid only on the 1st purchase of the referee of ₹99 or above.
2. Currently valid only in India.
3. Refer credit will be rewarded in the form of non-transferrable balance.
4. Only valid on purchase made through our WhatsApp portal.
